Transport Statistics

We just wanted a way to log our trips that wasn't full of ads or nonsense. It's a simple tool for keeping track of where you've been on buses and trains.

No more typing in every detail.

Since this pulls from bustimes.org and Network Rail, you don't have to manually type in the bus model or route info. You just pick the vehicle and the stops, and the site sorts the rest.

Maps & Data

It draws maps of your day out or your whole history. You can see what you've ridden most - like your top liveries and vehicle types - plus how much of a fleet or route you've actually completed.

"I was here"

If you're out with mates, you don't all have to log the same thing. If your friend logs a trip, you can just hit "I was here" on their profile and it'll jump onto your log too.

What we're working on

We're still tweaking bits of the site. Soon we'll be adding total mileage tracking and the total time you've spent out on the network.

Free and ad-free.

This is strictly for real-life travel. It's just an alternative for people who want to keep their logs organized without a headache.
