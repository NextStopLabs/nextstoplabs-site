Community Feedback: Completion Tracking

Route and fleet completion tracking is shaping up to be one of the most important features.

The goal is to allow users to:

  • Track which routes they've completed
  • Track which vehicles they've ridden
  • View operator-based completion stats

Future improvements may include:

  • Sorting fleets by vehicle number or usage
  • Tracking withdrawn vehicles (with some caveats due to transfers)
